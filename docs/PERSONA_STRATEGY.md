## Persona Deep Dives

### üéÆ Teen (15-18)

- **Core Need:** Feel like they're doing something cool, not "educational"
- **Where They Come From:** TikTok, YouTube, Discord servers
- **First Impression Must Be:** Interactive, visual, zero lecture vibes
- **Messaging Tone:** Casual, no corporate speak, emoji-friendly
- **Success Metric:** Daily active streak, social shares

### üíº Young Adult (18-25)

- **Core Need:** Tangible career advantage in AI economy
- **Where They Come From:** LinkedIn, Twitter/X, college career centers
- **First Impression Must Be:** "This can get me hired"
- **Messaging Tone:** Professional but not boring, ROI-focused
- **Success Metric:** Certificate completion, job matches viewed

### üë®‚Äçüë©‚Äçüëß Parent

- **Core Need:** Confidence that this is safe and worthwhile for their child
- **Where They Come From:** School emails, parent forums, word of mouth
- **First Impression Must Be:** Clear curriculum, safety features visible
- **Messaging Tone:** Trustworthy, educational, no hype
- **Success Metric:** Child engagement, parent dashboard usage

### üë©‚Äçüè´ Instructor

- **Core Need:** Tool that makes their job easier, not harder
- **Where They Come From:** Ed-tech conferences, teacher networks, pilot programs
- **First Impression Must Be:** "I can use this tomorrow"
- **Messaging Tone:** Practical, classroom-focused, time-saving
- **Success Metric:** Class enrollment, student progress rates

### üè¢ Program Director

- **Core Need:** Scalable solution with minimal risk
- **Where They Come From:** RFP processes, vendor demos, referrals
- **First Impression Must Be:** Enterprise-ready, compliant, supportable
- **Messaging Tone:** Professional, ROI-focused, risk-mitigating
- **Success Metric:** Pilot success, contract renewal

### üåü Reentry Trainee

- **Core Need:** Hope + practical skills without judgment
- **Where They Come From:** Workforce development programs, social services
- **First Impression Must Be:** Welcoming, supportive, achievable
- **Messaging Tone:** Encouraging, clear, human
- **Success Metric:** Course completion, job applications submitted

### üßí Youth Beginner (10-14)

- **Core Need:** Fun that doesn't feel like school
- **Where They Come From:** Parents, after-school programs, summer camps
- **First Impression Must Be:** Game-like, colorful, instant rewards
- **Messaging Tone:** Playful, exciting, zero friction
- **Success Metric:** Time spent, challenges completed
