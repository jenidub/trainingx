## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Words We Use

- **Empower** (not teach)
- **Practice** (not study)
- **Skills** (not knowledge)
- **Opportunities** (not jobs)
- **Guide** (not instruct)

### Words We Avoid

- "Easy" (implies it's trivial)
- "Just" (dismissive)
- "Obviously" (condescending)
- "Failure" (use "learning opportunity")
- "Test" (use "challenge")
