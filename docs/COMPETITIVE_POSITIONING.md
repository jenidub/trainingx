## Objection Handling

### "I can just use ChatGPT to learn"

> "Absolutely—and that's a great start. But ChatGPT doesn't tell you if you're getting better, doesn't give you certificates employers verify, and doesn't match you with jobs. It's like saying you can get fit by watching yourself in the mirror. You need feedback, progression, and proof."

### "There are free courses everywhere"

> "Free courses are great for awareness. But 95% of people never finish them, and a YouTube completion doesn't get you hired. TrainingX is designed for completion—gamification, Spiral support, and a certificate at the end that actually means something."

### "I don't have time for another platform"

> "That's exactly why we built the Practice Zone. 10 minutes a day builds real skills. We're not another course to add to your backlog—we're daily practice that compounds into career advancement."

### "Certificates don't matter"

> "Generic certificates don't. But verifiable skill demonstrations do. Our certificates link to the actual challenges you completed—employers can see exactly what you can do, not just what you watched."

### "AI is just a fad"

> "AI isn't going anywhere. The question is: do you want to be someone who uses AI, or someone who's replaced by people who do? We're not training you on a specific tool that might change—we're building fundamental prompting skills that transfer anywhere."
